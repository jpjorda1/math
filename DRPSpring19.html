Spring 2019-2020

  • Geometry & Differential Forms.
    This course will be an introduction to calculus on smooth manifolds by way of differential forms. We will start on Rn and then move to manifolds, where we will see Stoke's theorem and the Poincare lemma; hopefully reaching the Gauss-Bonnett Theorem and a theorem of Morse if we have time.
    We will be using Manfredo Do Carmo's Differential Forms & Applications. [.pdf]

    • Chapter 1: Differential Forms in Rn

    • Chapter 2: Line Integrals

    • Chapter 3: Differentiable Manifolds

    • Chapter 4: Integration on Manifolds; Stoke's Theorem and Poincaré's Lemma

    • Chapter 5: Differential Geometry of Surfaces

    • Chapter 6: The Theorem of Gauss-Bonnet and the Theorem of Morse
